## Required Preservation Rule

Every harvested source must be preserved before interpretation.

Required minimum metadata:

- Source title
- Source URL or file origin
- Date published or meeting date if available
- Date fetched or imported
- Local preserved path
- Source type
- Matter link
- Proof of Fact (Human plus Evidence)
- Factual Strength rating with rationale

## Do Not Do

- Do not treat starter chronology as verified source evidence.
- Do not delete duplicate source candidates automatically.
- Do not collapse advocacy documents into primary source material.
- Do not infer council fault from silence unless the duty, date and missing action are source-linked.
- Do not present automated status labels as findings.
